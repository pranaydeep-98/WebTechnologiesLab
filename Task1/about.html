Cherabuddi Education Society

The Cherabuddi Education Society aims at creating a state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of the twin states of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. The Society aims to harness technical excellence and financial power of NRIs in making CVR College of Engineering a pre-eminent technical institution.
